## 3. Backlink profile notes

- **El Prado** has the widest moat: **805 referring domains**, with elite citations from wikipedia.org, bing.com, forbes.com, wikivoyage.org plus Colombian press (las2orillas.co, colombia.travel, traveler.es) and Barranquilla institutions (probarranquilla.org, carnavaldebarranquilla.org, barranquilla.gov.co).
- **Dann Carlton** has fewer ref domains (207) but high AS (31); ~50% of its raw links come from sister-chain hotelesdann.com (872). Quality editorial links: elespectador.com, caracol.com.co, uninorte.edu.co, colombia.travel.
- A recurring, **earnable link pattern** appears across both: Colombian travel/tourism + local-institution sites (colombia.travel, probarranquilla.org, carnavaldebarranquilla.org, comisionfilmicacolombia.com, wanderlog/wikivoyage/sygic travel directories). These are realistic targets for a new Barahona 72 site.
- No obvious toxic/PBN patterns in the top 40 of either — these are legitimate profiles.

## 4. Battlefield — how hard, and where to win fastest

**Difficulty: moderate, not fortified.** Authority Scores cluster at **23–31** and backlink counts are in the hundreds-to-low-thousands — beatable with disciplined execution. Critically, **almost all competitor traffic is BRANDED**: Dann Carlton draws 57% from "hotel dann carlton barranquilla"; El Prado 48% from "hotel el prado barranquilla"; Estelar 75% from two "estelar" terms. The generic, non-brand local terms ("hotel barranquilla," "hotel prado 72," "pasadia barranquilla," "salon de eventos barranquilla," "rooftop barranquilla," "hoteles en el prado barranquilla") are held only **incidentally at positions 3–9, rarely #1** — they are under-optimized and contestable. The pure head term "hotel barranquilla" (4,400) is OTA-dominated (Booking/Expedia), so it's the hardest; skip it early.

**Fastest gaps for a brand-new optimized presence:**
1. **Own the brand first** — "barahona 72," "hotel barahona 72," "ayenda 1313 barranquilla": zero competition, instant once a site + Google Business Profile exist.
2. **Exploit the "Calle 72 / Prado 72" geography** — Barahona 72 sits on the exact street El Prado markets; "hotel prado 72" (2,400, only #3-defended) and "hoteles en el prado barranquilla" / "prado barranquilla" are the single most relevant, winnable cluster.
3. **Budget/business + daypass long-tail** — "hotel economico barranquilla," "hotel cerca a [landmark]," "pasadia barranquilla" (1,300, competitors only #5): low defense, matches the hotel's positioning.

**Reality check:** Sky Collection (AS 8, 540 traffic) proves a site alone isn't enough — content + Colombian travel/institution citations are required. With NO website today, Barahona 72 starts from zero; expect 4–6 months to build AS into the 15–25 range. Priority order: launch optimized site → claim/optimize GBP for the map pack (separate from these web metrics, fastest local ROI) → publish Prado-72 geo + daypass content → earn the recurring colombia.travel / probarranquilla / travel-directory citations the leaders share.
